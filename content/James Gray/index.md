I’m James. Welcome to my personal website and digital garden - [here’s a playlist](https://open.spotify.com/playlist/0N4UO38g2AjRHKH69CLUen?si=778a1303cac74733) you can enjoy while you’re here.

I like building things that address important problems. Recently, that’s manifested through my company [Seren Labs](https://www.serenlabs.io/) and [Bottlenecks Institute](https://www.bottlenecksinstitute.com/). I’m especially passionate about solving [[Climate Change]] and experimenting with ways to usher in a more agentic and abundant world. 

Much of my work revolves around data (Seren Labs & data.world), investigating solutions to mismatches between supply and demand (data.world & [SpareFoot](https://www.sparefoot.com/)), and supporting emerging technology & early-stage companies (Seren Labs & Austin Technology Incubator).

Here are a few throughlines in my work, a combination of things I’m interested in and particularly good at:
- Investigating barriers to progress and exploring novel ways to move past them
- Talking with people about things
- Making connections between different domains, and applying solutions from one field to another
- Building strong partnerships, communities & ecosystems
- Putting structure around ideas to scale them from 0 to 1
- Deep research & finding niche sources of data and information

You can see some of the things I've talked about publicly in [[Conversations & Presentations]]

In my spare time I manage a few farms in Indiana, read a lot of science fiction and longform articles/newsletters, and spend time with my wonderful family and friends in Austin.

Some of my interests: 
- Going to salon-style talks, and occasionally giving my own. Often with [Austin Salon](https://www.theaustinsalon.com/), [Tiny Talks](https://tinytalks.com/), [ATXplained](https://www.kut.org/atxplained), and [Pecha Kucha](https://www.instagram.com/pechakuchaatx/?hl=en).
- Reading, especially science fiction. 
- Getting immersed in nature; gardening & native plants, farming, hiking, and the [Wild Gift](https://www.wildgift.org/) community.
- Running (solo or with [Artist Run Club](https://northern-southern.com/artist-run-club/)) & [lifting heavy things](https://www.barbellmedicine.com/training-programs/strength/)
- Eating and cooking for friends & family, and sometimes with [Austin Supper Club](https://atxsupperclub.com/).
